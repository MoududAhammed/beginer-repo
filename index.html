Rangpur City:(রংপুর) is one of the main cities in Bangladesh. Rangpur was declared a district headquarters on 16th December in 1769. It established as a municipality in 1869. Rangpur City is one of the oldest municipalities of Bangladesh. The municipal staff building was elevated in 1892. It created under the priority Raja Janaki Ballay Sen.
